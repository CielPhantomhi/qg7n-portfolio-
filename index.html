About

Who I am

I'm Qg7n, a Roblox developer focused on SCP Foundations and roleplay games.

I've been developing in Roblox Studio for multiple years now, originally just messing around with personal projects, and then slowly joining other groups and helping out friends.

I work on full projects and games, or just small assets that need to be done.
